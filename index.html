About ISTE

The Indian Society for
Technical Education (ISTE)
is the leading National Professional
non-profit making Society for
the Technical Education System
in our country with the motto
of Career Development of Teachers
and Personality Development of Students
and overall development of our Technical
Education System operating in association with
All India Council for Technical Education
(AICTE) and other International Projects.
